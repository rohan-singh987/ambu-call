Ambu@Need- Kyuki apno se badh kar kuch bhi nhi!

Covid-19 has given all of you and us a hard hit. No one out there is unaware of the reality of agony faced by the whole globe. Fight for beds in the hospitals, unavailability of an ambulance at the need or shortage of medicines- all this has been a common scene during the ongoing pandemic. We also feel the pain of losing your beloved ones which increases because of the lack of reach of medicare facilities. We understand!


Therefore, here we are - Ambu@Need.


We have come up with the motive to help the community with our idea. If you are unsure of the availability of ambulances near you, Ambu@Need is the solution! If you do not have the information about how to call an ambulance at an urgent need, we are there for you. If you are unaware of the hospitals in your locality, contact us! We promise to be your aid till you find the proper medicare facilities.


Ambu@Need is an easy step solution to all these med worries. All you have to do is visit the website and enter your location. We know the medical availabilities of your city. A list of the available number of ambulances will be provided. Just select the one suited to you and go for the booking. The ambulance will be at your doorstep in 15 minutes at maximum. We know that every life is precious, and we care! We work with the aim that saving lives is the best contribution to humanity. We know that living in remote areas far away from hospitals is quirky. We feel your helplessness of the moment and that's why Ambu@Need is there for you- 24 x 7 at just one call!
